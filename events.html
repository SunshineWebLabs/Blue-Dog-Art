Lymington Art Exhibition “New Forest Ponds, Puddles & Paths”

When: Time and date will appear here

Where: Location will appear here

An exhibition of over 40 paintings, almost all of them new creations, by Pete Gilbert – well renowned Hampshire artist who is passionate about the New Forest. His paintings play with light and dark, shapes and form and create images of landscapes that we can relate to, that we can see, feel and smell the scent of morning or late afternoon, hear the bird calls, feel the squelchy mud and the prickles of the teasles! It promises to be a wonderful exhibition from 17th January to 28th February at Lymington’s St Barbe Museum & Art Gallery. Booking on line through the Museum.
